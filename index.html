Game 2

Get off my Lawn

ABOUT
Get off my lawn! is a 2D tower defence strategy game in which the player must defend their house against waves of enemy units, and strike back at the enemy base. The player does so by sending out units of their own to attack the enemy and building towers to defend against the enemy waves. The objective of the game is to hold out against all enemy waves, or to destroy the enemy base.
